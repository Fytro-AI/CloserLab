Frequently Asked Questions

Is an AI sales coach as good as a real coach?

For drilling specific skills at high volume — often better. A real coach is irreplaceable for strategy and nuanced feedback. But for reps on objection handling and cold calls, an AI available 24/7 beats a manager who can observe one call a week.

How does sales roleplay work in CloserLab?

You pick a scenario, the AI buyer starts the call, and you practice live. The AI responds dynamically to whatever you say — it's not a choose-your-own-adventure, it's a real conversation.

Can I practice with my voice?

Yes. CloserLab Pro includes Realtime AI Voice — you speak, the AI talks back. Highest-fidelity training mode because the real call is always voice.
