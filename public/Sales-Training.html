What Is Sales Training Software?

Sales training software helps reps develop skills outside of live customer interactions. Traditional training uses videos, scripts, and manager role-plays. Modern AI-powered training — like CloserLab — simulates realistic buyer conversations so reps can practice objection handling, cold calls, discovery, and closing in a safe environment that actually feels like the real thing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CloserLab free?

Yes. CloserLab has a free tier that lets you run practice simulations with no credit card required. Paid plans unlock unlimited simulations, voice AI calls, and advanced analytics.

How realistic is the AI buyer?

Very. The AI is trained to behave like specific buyer personas — skeptical, aggressive, distracted, budget-conscious, time-starved. It interrupts, objects, and responds to what you actually say, not a script.

What industries does it cover?

SaaS, real estate, recruiting, consulting, e-commerce, agency, healthcare, coaching, and more. You can also enter a custom industry description for your specific niche.

Can I use it for interview practice?

Yes — CloserLab has a dedicated Interview Prep mode where an AI hiring manager conducts a realistic SDR/BDR/AE interview and scores you on what real interviewers care about.

Does it work for teams?

Yes. Sales leaders can use CloserLab to give their reps a structured training tool that tracks performance, identifies skill gaps, and keeps reps sharp between coaching sessions.
