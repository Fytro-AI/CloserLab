The Objections You'll Face

"We're happy with what we have."

The most common cold call killer. Practice reframing without being pushy.

"Just send me an email."

The polite brush-off. Learn to stay on the call without sounding desperate.

"We don't have budget right now."

Budget objections in the first 30 seconds. Handle them without cutting price.

"Who gave you my number?"

The hostile opener. Practice staying calm and professional when it starts rough.

"I only have 2 minutes."

Time pressure from the start. Get to value fast without skipping the rapport.
