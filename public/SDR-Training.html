Why SDR Training Is Broken

Most SDR training: watch a few call recordings, shadow a senior rep for a week, then get thrown on the phones. The feedback loop is slow — you make a mistake on a real call, your manager hears about it days later in a 1-on-1, and by then you've made it fifty more times.

CloserLab closes that loop. Practice on AI buyers, get scored immediately, run another rep five minutes later. The feedback is instant, the stakes are zero, and the reps are unlimited.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CloserLab only for SDRs?

No — built for anyone in sales. SDRs and BDRs get the most value from cold call and meeting setter practice. AEs benefit from discovery and objection handling. Candidates use it for interview prep.

How is this different from practicing with a colleague?

A colleague goes easy on you. They accept weak answers because they don't want to be rude. CloserLab's AI pushes exactly as hard as the scenario demands, every time, with no social contract.

Can I practice for a specific industry?

Yes. Industry-specific buyer contexts for SaaS, real estate, recruiting, consulting, e-commerce, healthcare, and more. Plus a custom description field for your exact niche.

Is voice practice available?

Yes — CloserLab Pro includes Realtime AI Voice. The AI speaks, you speak back. Highest-fidelity training mode because it mirrors the actual call experience.
